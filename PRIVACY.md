# Privacy Policy – Kiping

**Last updated:** July 15, 2026

Kiping is a lightweight financial companion for micro and small business owners. It is designed to work simply, privately, and without requiring an account.

## Information We Collect

Kiping does **not** require you to create an account.

You do not need to provide:

- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Location
- Login credentials

Kiping does not use:

- Advertising services
- Analytics tools
- Tracking technologies
- Data brokers

## Your Financial Data

Your financial information, including:

- Transactions
- Business goals
- Task lists

is stored **locally on your device**.

The developer does **not** collect, receive, or store this information on any server.

## AI-Powered Financial Insights

When you request AI-generated financial insights, Kiping securely sends the financial information necessary to generate those insights to **Anthropic's Claude API** over an encrypted internet connection.

Depending on the requested analysis, this may include:

- Transactions
- Categories
- Cash flow summaries
- Business goals

This information is transmitted **only** to generate the financial insights you requested.

The developer does **not** store this transmitted financial data on its own servers.

Data processed through Anthropic's Claude API is subject to Anthropic's own privacy practices.

For more information:

- Anthropic Privacy Policy: https://www.anthropic.com/legal/privacy
- Anthropic Commercial Terms: https://www.anthropic.com/legal/commercial-terms

## Data Storage and Deletion

All app data is stored locally on your device.

You can delete individual:

- Transactions
- Goals
- Tasks

at any time within the app.

Uninstalling Kiping normally removes its locally stored app data from your device, subject to your device's operating system and backup settings.

## Exporting Data

If you choose to export your data, Kiping creates a file on your device and opens your device's built-in sharing options.

Kiping does not receive, store, or control your exported data after you choose where to save or share it.

## Internet Usage

Kiping works offline for its core features, including:

- Transaction recording
- Goal tracking
- Task management
- Financial summaries

An internet connection is required only when you request AI-powered financial insights.

## Third-Party Services

Kiping currently uses the following third-party service:

- Anthropic Claude API, used solely to generate AI-powered financial insights upon user request.

Kiping does **not** use:

- Advertising SDKs
- Analytics services
- User tracking technologies
- Data brokers

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

If future versions of Kiping change how data is collected, stored, processed, or shared, this Privacy Policy will be updated before or alongside the release of those changes.
